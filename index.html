Downtown Morgan Hill Preschool

Opening in April 2024

We are opening a new preschool in the heart of downtown Morgan Hill. We've crafted classroom environments with big outdoor play areas, a perfect place for young children to create, explore, connect, and discover.

We will offer infant care as well as preschool for children 2 months to 6 years old. Our staff is highly qualified in child development and education, with the majority of our teachers working for more than a decade.

As a school focused on the learning process, we strive to develop the whole child with opportunities in language, math, science, music, social interaction and much more. With our approach, parents can rest assured that the safety and care of their child is being met in a prepared environment, with dedicated staff, and opportunities to grow as a learner.


Enrollment begins in March and we will be opening our preschool in April of 2024. Join the waitlist today to learn more about the location and schedule a tour.
